Hall 入殿

Take a 3-minute pause

A simple guided reset for anyone. No background required.

Practice
  1. Inhale slowly for four counts.
  2. Exhale slowly for six counts.
  3. Repeat ten times. Let the mind soften.
Timer: 03:00

Tip: use this before prayer, study, or a difficult conversation.

Dharma 明白意义

Spiritual ideas, explained simply

Each topic answers three questions: what it is, what it is not, and how to try it today.

Explainer • 3 min

What is prayer?

What it is: A way to set intention and remember what matters.

What it is not: Asking for miracles or pleasing a higher power.

Try this: For one minute, silently wish something good for yourself and one other person.

Explainer • 3 min

What is dedication (回向)?

What it is: Sharing the benefit of a calm or kind moment with others.

What it is not: A rule, a debt, or a religious obligation.

Try this: After a pause, think of one person and wish them ease.

Explainer • 3 min

Why light a lamp?

What it is: A symbolic reminder of clarity and care.

What it is not: Worship of an object or belief in special powers.

Try this: As you light a lamp, take one steady breath and name a quality you want to carry.
